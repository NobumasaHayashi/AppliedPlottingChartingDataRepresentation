Toronto Maple Leafs Statistics and History

Leagues -> NHL -> Toronto Maple Leafs
The Toronto Maple Leafs are a Major League hockey team based in Toronto, ONT playing in the National Hockey League from 1927 to 2021. The team played in the Toronto Arena Gardens, among others.
